Action Packed Yogurt Protein Bowl

It is time to do this thing. I don't think you are even ready for the amount of carbs in this dish

Ingredientes

  • 950 grams of Fage yogurt
  • 4 cups of whole grain, rolled oats
  • Organic pasta de mani, sin azucar
  • Chia seeds, flax seeds, cocoa nibs, hemp seeds, and pumpkin seeds
  • Blueberries, one banana, and blackberries
  • Coconut oil (1 table spoon)

Instruciones

  1. 4 cups of water, preferably high quality water. If it ain't, that's fine, hopefully the bad shit will get filtered out during the boiling
  2. Bring the water to a boil in a big pot with a sprinkle of Himalayan Pink Sea Salt if you're boujee. Add oats, reduce the heat to low, and cap with a lid.
  3. Stir often and keep an eye on them. The starch forms a foam that cannot be stopped once it rises, so you may have to alternate the heat or bat it down with a spoon. Seriously, otherwise, your stovetop will get destroyed and the hardened starch is a bear to remove
  4. While the oatmeal does its thing, get the rest of your ingredientes out and locked and loaded
  5. Add 300-400 grams of yogurt to a large bowl, or half of the container. Add three tablespoons of mani de pasta, and the remainder of the ingredientes, EXCEPT the coconut oil.
  6. Add oatmeal to the bowl when it's ready, straining out all of the water. Add a table spoon of coconut oil to the hot oatmeal on top, before thoroughly mixing the bowl for roughly one minutes
  7. Garnish with cinnamon and enjoy!

You better be hungry. Definitely add enough chia seeds to balance out the carb load. It can be a lot if it's your first time
