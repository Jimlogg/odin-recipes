Steak n' Eggs

Elevate your masculinity with this nourishing, protein-packed meal. Accompanied by soft, flavorful scrambled eggs, this pound of mouth-watering grass-fed beef is sure keep you full for the day

Ingredientes

  • One pound of ribeye steak
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of ghee butter
  • Herbs of your choice
  • One avocado
  • One cup of blueberries

Instrucciones

  1. Cast iron skillset to medium-high
  2. Season steak how you prefer on a cuttingboard
  3. Add a tablespoon of ghee to the pan once hot, and lay steak away from you unto the pan
  4. Turn every 45 seconds, about 6 times
  5. Crack 6 eggs into hot pot with 1 teaspoon of butter
  6. Scramble with wooden spoon, alternating heat between on and off every 30 seconds to prevent burning, and add herbs and spices
  7. Remove steak, and let sit while the eggs finish
  8. Cut up avocado, and place unto plate alongside the blueberries
  9. Place steak and eggs on plate too, and enjoy!
