Taco Mode

Who doesn't like tacos, am I right?

Ingredientes

  • 2 pounds of chicken breasts
  • Tortillas
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 limes
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 peppers, whichever color prefieres
  • 9 garlic cloves
  • Seasoning of your choice

Instruciones

  1. Season chicken, cut up into smaller pieces, throw on a medium skillet with olive oil and flip as necessary
  2. While chicken is cooking, chop up onion and pepper to carmelize on a low-medium pan with one teaspoon of ghee butter, olive oil, and spices. Leave red pepper for the guac
  3. Add 5 cloves of garlic to onion and pepper skilllet
  4. Cut and smash up avocado in bowl. Add lime, spices, and red onion. A little salt and pepper, too
  5. Heat up tortillas in toaster oven or toaster if you have one
  6. Remove chicken when ready, and keep onion+peppers on low
  7. Add chicken, guac, and onion+pepper combo to the toasted tortillas, garnish with some lime, and enjoy!
